Discover Khajjiar

Nature’s Canvas

Khajjiar is a breathtaking plateau nestled amidst the Dhauladhar ranges of the Himalayas. Its vast green meadow, mirrored lake, and forested backdrop create a picture-perfect landscape often compared to Switzerland. The air is crisp, the pine scent calming — a true Alpine escape in India.

Plan Your Visit

Best visited from March to June and September to December. Nearest airport: Gaggal (120 km). Khajjiar is easily accessible from Dalhousie and Chamba by road. Experience horse riding, nature walks, or simply unwind amidst the whispering pines.
