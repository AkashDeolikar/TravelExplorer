Why Visit Madla?

Nestled along the tranquil banks of the Ken River, Madla is a meeting point of mountains, rivers, and forests — a rare confluence of natural harmony. The village offers sustainable homestays, rural art, and slow living experiences surrounded by pristine wilderness.

Source: Incredible India – Madla

Plan Your Visit

Madla is best visited between October and March. From here, you can reach Khajuraho’s temples or explore Panna National Park for its tigers and waterfalls — all while experiencing Bundelkhand’s warmth and heritage.
